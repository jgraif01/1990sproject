Here's a quick summary of Quake; This is a first person shooter game that has influences from Doom with similar gameplay, design and story. The player takes the role of a unnamed protagonist who is sent into a portal to stop an enemy code-named Quake. Players have to find their way through many challenges and levels in a medieval environment while battling monsters using different sorts of weaponry. The game consists of 38 seperate levels divided into 7 episodes, each single player represents individual and different dimensions that the player an access through some magic portals.
