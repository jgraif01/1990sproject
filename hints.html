Quake Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips for playing Quake! Source: thegamer.com

  • Practice with bots
  • Become familiar with maps
  • Win the Mad Dash
  • Stay on the move
  • Circle around your enemies
  • Predict other players' moves
  • Learn to rocket jump
  • Take out respawning players/camp
  • Know when to leave a fight
  • Don't be afraid to die
